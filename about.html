way of working

Learn about how Dariyaa works

The massive migration to urban settlements in search of employment opportunities and the disparity in accessibility of quality and relevant education across institutions in the country brought together few individuals to create a model for facilitating and strengthening the education system. This model involved assessment of educational level, requirement and gap analysis, relevant institution and course mapping, scholarships, support and care. As the model entered its 7th year with its first batch of class 10 students, a possibility to form a not-for-profit organization emerged. In 2016, “Dariyaa Social Welfare Society” was incorporated. However, it began its journey back in 2010, by organizing campaigns for voluntary teaching in basic literacy and elementary education in parts of Noida and Ghaziabad, with the participation of students, corporate employees, retired professionals, school teachers, homemakers, a few religious and educational institutions. Since its inception, Dariyaa has supported the enrollment and engagement of children in formal schools. During this process, the growing need for relevant skill training and livelihood opportunities for women was realized and Dariyaa launched its livelihoods program in Sustainable Craft in 2017. Over the years, Dariyaa Society has evolved as an institution that provides a platform for students, professionals, artists, other institutions and business organizations to collaborate and contribute towards the sustainable evolution of education system and economic empowerment.
